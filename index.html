Terminology


  • MSRP
    Manufacturer’s Suggested Retail Price
  • Capitalized Cost
    The negotiated selling price of the vehicle, plus other costs included in the lease, such as acquisition fee, title and registration fees, insurance, etc.
  • Cap Cost Reduction
    An amount (net trade-in allowance, rebate, or cash) applied that reduces the capitalized cost, resulting in lower monthly payments
  • Depreciation
    The amount of a vehicle’s projected decline in value through normal use during the lease term
  • Money Factor
    The cost of financing a lease
  • Residual
    The estimated value of a leased vehicle at the end of the lease
  • Term
    The number of months allotted in a contracted lease agreement
  • Closed-end Lease
    A type of lease in which the lessor absorbs the loss if, at the end of the lease, the vehicle is worth less than the residual value. Lessee is responsible for any excess mileage and/or excess wear and use charges
