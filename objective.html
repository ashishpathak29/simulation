Welcome

Hello and welcome to the Advanced Leasing Strategies Simulation. In this training, you have an opportunity to practice presenting the leasing option to a cash customer. Offering lease options to cash customers can be challenging. They are often motivated by different things, including the convenience of never having to pay a monthly invoice, not wanting to pay finance charges, or simply choosing to own the vehicle outright.

This exercise is meant to simulate an actual conversation between you and a customer interested in purchasing a Mercedes-Benz vehicle. You will be able to apply what you learn and help your customer make the finance decision best-suited for their needs.

  • You will not receive immediate feedback as to whether your responses are "right" or "wrong." Instead, you will have to look for verbal and non-verbal cues from your customer to identify how you are doing as you go along. Your progress will be determined by the choices you make.
  • You will receive detailed feedback on your performance at the end of the simulation, including information on which stages you have mastered and which you may want to review.

The flow presented here provides the framework for how you will work through this customer conversation.
